About this project.

I'm developing a virtual library application to manage and track my physical book collection. The app lets me digitally organize my books, update their status, and monitor my reading progress and knowledge.

The project is still under development, and more details will be added as it progresses. In the meantime, you can check out the early version here: cmrabdu.com/My-virtual-library.
